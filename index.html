about us

Bangladesh Secretariate Clinic was established aiming to serve health care for the officers and thousands of staff working in the secretariat. From its inception, it has been working as the only health provider in the secretariat. Various types of health services include emergency first aid, outpatient services, specialist consultant OPD service, and most important provided almost all medicines prescribed by the physician. It also arranges emergency vaccination programs during Hajj. Emergency service on spot was also provided from the clinic. Various diagnostic facilities are also provided. Though there are no in-patient facilities, serious patients are carried out to the specialized hospital through ambulance service.
